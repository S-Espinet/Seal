All about Seal

Seal is an R&B Artist. He started his career in 1991. Seal is most well known for songs like "A Kiss From A Rose", "Crazy", "Get It Together" and "Human Beings". Seal was married to Heidi Klum and he has also been covered by other famous artists such as Jack Black.
